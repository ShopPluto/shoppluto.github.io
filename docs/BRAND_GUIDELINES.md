# 🚀 Pluto Brand Guidelines

Version: 1.0

---

# Brand Name

Pluto

---

# Tagline

Smart Shopping. Infinite Choices.

---

# Brand Mission

To provide a premium, trustworthy and intelligent online shopping experience for everyone.

---

# Brand Vision

Become one of the most loved digital commerce platforms by combining technology, design and customer satisfaction.

---

# Brand Values

- Trust
- Simplicity
- Innovation
- Speed
- Quality
- Customer First

---

# Brand Personality

Pluto should always feel:

- Premium
- Modern
- Friendly
- Intelligent
- Professional
- Reliable

Never:

- Cheap
- Cluttered
- Confusing
- Outdated

---

# Logo Guidelines

The logo should be:

- Simple
- Memorable
- Scalable
- Modern

Preferred styles:

- Flat Design
- Minimal Design

Avoid:

- Complex gradients
- Heavy shadows
- Clipart
- 3D effects

---

# Color Personality

Primary Blue

Represents

- Trust
- Stability
- Technology

Purple

Represents

- Innovation
- Creativity

Cyan

Represents

- Energy
- Freshness

---

# Typography Personality

Headings

Bold

Clean

Confident

Body Text

Readable

Professional

Friendly

---

# Photography Style

Use:

- High-quality product images
- White or clean backgrounds
- Consistent lighting
- Premium presentation

Avoid:

- Low-resolution images
- Watermarks
- Busy backgrounds

---

# Icon Style

Minimal

Rounded

Modern

Consistent line thickness

---

# Illustration Style

Simple

Flat

Modern

Minimal

---

# Tone of Voice

Professional

Friendly

Helpful

Confident

Never:

- Aggressive
- Misleading
- Overpromising

---

# User Experience Principles

- Easy to Navigate
- Fast Loading
- Mobile Friendly
- Accessible
- Consistent

---

# Future Expansion

The Pluto brand should support:

- Marketplace
- Mobile App
- AI Shopping Assistant
- Seller Portal
- Admin Dashboard
